Vaccinise helps you track & manage your child's vaccination schedule.

  • Generates personalized vaccine schedule.
  • Gives periodic reminders about upcoming inoculations.
  • Ideal for today's parents.
  • Provides information about nearby vaccination centres.
  • Get Vaccinise from Google Play.

Features

Periodic Reminders

Vaccinise periodically reminds you of upcoming vaccination dates.

Generates Schedule

Automatically generates personalized vaccination schedule once biographical data of the child is entered.

Multi-user Support

You can use Vaccinise to keep track of vaccination schedules for multiple children.

Nearby Vaccination Centres (Beta)

Vaccinise provides the location and contact information of nearby vaccination centres.

Worldwide Support

Vaccinise's data is compatible with vaccination & immunization standards around the world.

Replacement

Vaccinise aims to replace existing paper-based vaccination cards that are hard to manage, with a simple mobile app. Yes, it's an ambitious goal.

Frequently Asked Questions

How will this app send me notifications?

Vaccinise uses Android's notification system to automatically notify you about upcoming inoculations.

How to see if a vaccine has been given already?

If a vaccine has already been administered, the color changes to gray. To reverse the color back, double tap on it.

Is my personal data secure?

Vaccinise encrypts and stores all the data that is entered/produced on your device itself. Thus, there's no risk of your precious data leaking anywhere.

How can I contribute to Vaccinise?

We are continuously looking for support in developing Vaccinise. If you would like to contribute as a developer or would like to donate money/resources, drop us an email.

Why did you develop Vaccinise?

To take a look at why we developed Vaccinise, see our story.

How much does Vaccinise cost?

Vaccinise is 100% free for personal use.
